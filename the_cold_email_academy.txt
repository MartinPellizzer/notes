- write irresistible cold emails
	- one goal: simple response (setup call, link, etc...)
	- coincise, clear, casual
	- highlight benefits, not features
	- short email (2-5) sentences
	- max 2 senteces together
	- put a questionmark (only one)

	- format:
		- build rapport + personalization + common thread
		- what you offer + value proposition
		- connect the dots, highlight a problem, outline a dream
		- call to action

	- personalization:
		- higher response rate to identify opportunity
		- refer to one of their latest actions (achievements? post?)
	
	- automation:
		- software or outsource

	- call to action:
		- have them either say YES or NO
		- "sounds good?", "are you open for a 15 minutes call?"

	- composing emails:
		- use small screens to write the email (mobal device)

	- subject line:
		- 3-6 words
		- reference, source, praise, +formula
		- benefits or curiosity, inform or personalized

	- dos and donts
		- use question mark to cta
		- use numbers (data and percetages)
		- never use ALL CAPS
		- never use re. or forward
		- no hype

	- open and closes
		- no formal heading
		- just a thank you, not "sencirely" etc...
		- say it like a friend

	- using ps. btw and fyi
		- put scarcety, curiosity, cool stats...
	
	- signature
		- 2 dashes, title, name, company address, website, calendar link, cool post, 
		- plain and clean

	- spot checking your emails
		- read aloud, must sound natural
		- look at it on mobile devices

	- compliance
		- no deceiving subject line
		- be clear on what you offer
		- make sure they have a way to opt out
