meeting, establishing rapport, communication
the greeting (The Warrior Greeting)
	open, eye, beam, hi, lean
	open: body language
	eye: be first with eye contact
	beam: be the first to smile
	hi!: say it with pleasing tonality, identify yourself first
	lean: take the lead. extend your hand. lean almost impreceptibly.
	(handshake): firm and respectful
	(hands-free greeting): do everything but the handshake (works great with groups or audience)
establishing rapport
	common ground, confort zone
	assertiveness, warmth, humor
	synchronize body language, voice of tone, conversation skill, discover if target is visual, auditory, kin
