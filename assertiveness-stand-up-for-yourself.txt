2. HOW DO OTHERS SEE YOU?
-----------------------
body language
posture, gestures, eye movement and other facial expressions
weak body language VS strong body language

eyes
	eye contact
	practice with mirror
	notice your eye color

smile
	no huge toothy grin or a tight smirk

shoulders
	- back and relaxed
	- open up your chest
	- shrug and roll

posture
	- stand and sit well
	- stand erect with head up
	- sit straight and tall, feet on ground
	- feet slightly apart and facing forward

hands
	- never point to people
	- use occasional hand gesture comfortably
	- hold a piece of paper or a pen if you have problems controlling your hands

voice
	- tone

words
	- basic assertion, meet your own needs first
	- empathy, "i understand that ___, but ___. So i need to ___"
	- escalate, become increasingly firm while remaining calm and in control (don't escalate tone or mood)
	- "i" or "me", "i feel like you are being rude", "to me, it seems you don't listen"

practice
	- mirror


3. PUT YOUR PLAN TO WORK IN YOUR PERSONAL RELATIONSHIPS
-----------------------
1. take back the power
2. determine what you want
3. communicate your want
4. continue to be caring

parenting children
	- express respect and love for you children
	- tell them what is going wrong and why you're not happy with them
	- tell them specifically what you expect them to do and how to treat you
	- explain the consequences if they can't comply with your rules
	- finally, actively listen to your children so they can explain their side
	- then you can open the discussion up further to come to an agreement together
