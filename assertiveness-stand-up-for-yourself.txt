SELF-EVALUATION QUIZ
-----------------------

1. do you make eye contact when talking to people? tell me eye color?
2. do people tell you to speak more loudly or to repeat yourself often?
3. do you speak confidently and without "ums" and "uhs"?
4. do you slouch or look down with bad posture?
5. do you ask questions when you need clarification?
6. do you feel comfortable around others?
7. do you say "no" when you don't want to do something?
8. do you ecpress annoyance or anger appropriately?
9. do you offer an opinion when you don't agree with someone?
10. do you defend yourself against mistakes that aren't your fault?
