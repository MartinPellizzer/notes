what to do before you start writing

	- writing about it is the easy part

	- in the land of the blind, the one-eyed man is king
	- there's no problem for which a great sales letter can't be the solution



PT.2 THE KENNEDY SYSTEM
==============================================
a system is a process, formula, framework - not creativity

CH.1 The clay with which to mold
	- STEP 1: get into the customer
		- to persuade someone, you need to understand that person
			1. what keeps them awake at night?
			2. what are they afraid of?
			3. what/who they are angry at?
			4. top 3 frustrations?
			5. what trends are occurring in their business?
			6. what do they secretly, ardently desire most?
			7. build-in bias?
			8. do they have their own language?
			9. who else is selling somenthing similar to them? how?
			10. who else has tried selling them them something similar?
		- how an outsider becomes and insider? (dentist example)
			1. read every industry publication every month
			2. visit websites that host discussions forums for dentists
			3. subscribe to e-mail gourps where only dentists communicate
			4. attend industry functions, conventions, seminars, shows
			5. 'play prospect' with other product and sevice providers
			6. mastermind with dentists and other marketers in industry
		- swim with the current, not against it
	- STEP 2: get into the offer
		- try out the product
		- ask testimonials
		- list feature and benifit, organize by importance
		- look for hidden benefits
	- STEP 3: create a damaging admission and address flaws openly
		- acknowledge the flaws of your product
		- address them as questions, objections, and concerns
		- figure out why they won't respond
			- objections, concerns, fears, doubts, excuses
			- use 3-by-5-inch cards
		- honestly assess the disadvantage of your offer and face them
			- nothing is perfect and everybody knows it
			- openly admit that and you increase credibility

CH.3 Eyes wide shut
	- STEP 6: get your sales letter read
		- AIDA
		- from annoying perst ot quite welcome guest
			- the right message, to the right people, at the right time
			- benefits, not gimmicks
			- headling, right after "Dear Mr. Buyer,"
		- headlines:
			- they didn't think i could ___, but i did.
			- who else wants ___?
			- how ___ made me ___
			- are you ___?
			- how i ___
			- how to ___
			- secrets of ___
			- Thousands (hundreds) now ___ even though they ___
			- warning: ___
			- give me ___ and i'll ___
			- [x] ways to ___
		- guidelines for mailing to executives and business owners:
			- use superior quality paper and envelopes (texture)
			- put your order form inside a second sealed package
			- incorporate prestige appeals in your sales letter
			  with word like: association, exclusive [see book]
		- speaking of grabbing attention
			- slap a penny to grab attention
			- make the email lumpy
			- trash can full of peanuts
			- [book]
		- tips for mailing to the mass market
			- people have zero attention these days
			- [book]
		- tips for mailing to sell products directly
			1. testimonals
			2. photographs outperform drawings and illustration
			3. prove ease of use
		- tips for mailing to sell professional sevices
			- credibility is key
			- number of years in business, numbers of clients
			  served, sample client list
			- believability is even more important than credibility
			- benefits realized, skepticism erased
			- facts and credibility only support persuasion
			- offer a free initia consultation of free info
			- before/after photographs
			- slice-of-life stories

CH.4 i want it, but not that much (price)
	- STEP 7: beat the bugaboo
		- sell bulk
		- discuss the price paid develop the offer
			- "we spent $3 million worth of research so you dont"
		- make the parts worth more than the whole
			- divide the product in components and sell bundle
			- price each component
		- installments?
		- three letter formulas that let you transcend price questions
			- PAS (problem-agitation-solution)
				- identify problem (real quantifyable problems)
				- inject emotion (agitation)
