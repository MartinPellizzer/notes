[summary]

visible in the feed:
- recent tweet from someone you follow
- popular tweet from someone you follow
- recent and popular replies from someone you follow
- tweet liked by people you follow
- trending tweet in a topic you follow

the plan to boost your growth:
- build relationships
- optimize for trending status

the 4-step formula:
- engage with others
- tweet at the right time and frequency
- make your tweets trending
- reply to everyone

engage with others:
- build relationships
- get priority in people's feed
- more vidibility = more engagement = more chance to be trending

tweet timing & frequency:
- tweet 5-7 days a week
- tweet between 1 and 4 times per day
- leave 2h between tweets
- tweet around best time to tweet
- stop replying to other people's tweets at least 1h before your tweets come out

to get a trending tweet:
- get 15-30 likes in first hour depending on niche
- use an exact word from the topic list inside the tweet
- add next to image & media tweets
- #hashtags are irrelevant

reply to everyone:
- convert more people into followers
- keep your tweets vidible longer
- try using people's first name
- hide negative, irrelevant or promotional replies

follow 50-200 people who:
- are in the same niche as you
- tweet everyday
- same language as you
- have similar number of followers as you
- inspire you

unfollow or mute people who:
- aren't related to your niche
- aren't inspiring you

groundwork:
- create 2 lists: peers and big accounts
- use list feeds or engagement builder to interract

find people to follow:
- go to trending tweets in your niche
- use recommended follows
- look at who they follow

good profile:
- real name and profile picture
- bio to establish credibility, satnd out, and give a reason to follow
- pinned tweet should add value, not promote
- no more than 1 or 2 links

how much time to spend:
- between 30 minutes and 2 hours per day

consistency:
- 5+ days a week
- better to spend 5x25 per week than 1x2h

[llama life]
spend your time this way:
- 35% of your time replying to peers list
- 15% of your time replying to big accounts list
- 20% of your time replying to notifications
- 30% of your time writing tweets
- optionally, add time to create unique content
- when you have more time, spend time replying to more people
