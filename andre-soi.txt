the art of preselling
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
the difference between 1-3% conversion and 5-20% of conversion is PRESELLING
preselling != selling
during preselling, there's no buy button. they are not even aware there's something to sell.
maybe use a presell site (PS) (multi-step)



framework of a presell
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- hook
- authority
- preeminence
- context
- consistency
- chain of beliefs
- fascination
- educational content



the hook (big idea)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

the big idea is a new way of saying something that has already been said
strong, unique, compelling
give them something they don't know

andre uses images for headlines (no stock image) because it's hard to come up with a great headline.
screenshots from magazines, give age to images in photoshop
picture of people experiencing the results is an example

find your copy buddy



authority
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
authority triggers:
	1. title
	2. chlotes
	3. trapping (accessories)

preeminence:
	- you become the only viable solution
	- you're not going to wait for money to change hands before you start contributing

multi-step presell site:
	- story-driven educational content to establish authority
	- then chan of beliefs down the line
	- you don't say you are an expert, you demonstrate it


context
	- first impression matters
	- use a powerful "first heard story" to create context (framing)

commitment and consistency
	- chain of yes. from low commitment to high commitment

educational content
	- useful but not complete
	- lust ends when craving ends
	- teach them what and why, sell them how
	- give them easy wins and sell them big ones

primary purpose of presell sites and worlds
	- be polarized, attract your tribe and repell all the others
	- repel the enemy, attract those who align with your values
	- go narrow and deep

the flow of a presell site
	- the ultimate goal is to build an email list

presell world structure
	- different ps, different experiences, different hero's journey, same destination
	- your customer is the hero, and the hero is flawed
	- the elephant and the blind men analogy
	- don't overwhelm them on first interraction, change their beliefs gently
	- don't shatter their entire worldview too soon or you will alienate them

build a presell site
	- minimalist
	- no distraction
	- one middle column
	- step-by-step: page 1 > page 2 > page 3...
	- ask for emails sign ups
