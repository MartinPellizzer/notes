discounting without damage
	- any reduction in prices can damage your price integrity
	- discounts devalue the product
	- so always give a reason for a discount and that your rules are solid
	- discounts can lead your clients to ask themselves why your price can be discounted
	- volume discount is fine
	- prepay discounts and bundles
	- specific time of year (off-season)
	- to get new customers, offer discount on only one type of product, to mitigate resentment
	- beware of free, free begets more free
	- erode trust, exposed,
	- don't give away for free something you priced premium before
	- give away highlights instead
	- aim for trust and loyalty, not sowing the seeds of hatred
	- discounts and free are powerful but can backfire
	- give for free informations to educate your market to buy your product
	- never
		- if you have a prestige offering, do not give it away for free, and beware of effering discount
		- if you must discount, must put items on sale or must promote free offers, don't be predictable

if and when you discount, get quid pro quo
