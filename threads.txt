7 disgusting techniques narcissists use to make kind people go insane.

Here they are:

- THREAD -

1. Gaslighting

Narcissists love lying, denying, and twisting the truth to make you doubt your own sanity.

Examples:
	- "I didn't do that, you're imagining things."
	- "You're crazy if you think that actually happened."
	- "That's not what I said, you must have misunderstood."


2. Blame Shift

Narcissists can't take responsibility and are really good at the blame game.

Examples:
	- "You always find a way to ruin things for us."
	- "You're always looking for something to blame me for."
	- "I wouldn't have done that if you didn't make me so angry."


3. Guilt Tripping

Narcissists surely know how to make you feel guily even when you've done nothing wrong.

Examples:
	- "I can't believe you would do this to me."
	- "Don't you see how much I've done for you?"
	- "Fine, do what makes YOU happy, but don't expect any support from ME."


4. Playing The Victim

Narcissists can't bear you are suffering more then them if it means stealing them the spotlight.

Examples:
	- "Nobody understands how hard it is for me."
	- "I'm the one who's suffering here, not you."
	- "All I ever do is give and give, and no one appreciates it."


5. Silent Treatment

Narcissits enjoy making you feel worthless by ignoring and ghosting you.

Examples:
	- Ignore calls and texts for days on end.
	- Give one-word answers or grunt in response to questions.
	- Walk out of the room mid-conversation without explanation.


6. Love Bombing

Narcissists make you feel special to create dependence and gain control over you.

Examples:
	- "No one understans me the way you do."
	- "I've never felt this way about anyone before."
	- "I can't believe how lucky I am to have found you."

7. Triangulation

Narcissists purposely create tension, confusion, and uncertainty by playing people against each others.

Examples:
	- "They're not as important to me as you are."
	- "I don't know why they keep trying to come between us."
	- "They told me something terrible about you, but I defended you."
