01. SOCIAL PROOF
	- never enough
	- testimonials
	- sprinkle them everywhere
	- avatar 1 -> objection 1
	- avatar 2 -> objection 2
	- avatar 3 -> objection 3
	- text, screenshot, videos...
	- layout:
		- headline
		- content
		- picture
		- name

02. GUARANTEE
	- absolute must
	- it addresses the biggest objection of all: 
		- fear, risk, uncertanty

03. MODULE BREAKDOWN (#6)
	- like table of contents for a course, but not quite
	- module 1 is..., module 2 is..., etc...
	- module headline + bullet list
	- divide your offer in smaller deliverables, and define them as modules
	- suppose each module is a book... would you buy that module by the headline only?
	- use action words in the title (e.g. activate, discover, remove...)
	- maybe add testimonials related to each module
	- bullets:
		- "discover how to ___ so that ___"
		- "the secret to ___"
		- "how to ___ without ___"
		- "the sneaky ___"
		- "the quickest/easiest/fastest ___"
		- "the single most ___"
		- "the truth about ___"
		- "what ___ don't tell you about ___"
	- the power of curiosity:
		- "the one secret..."
		- "the simple strategy on page 24 that will..."
