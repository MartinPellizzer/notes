=======================
FREE YOURSELF FROM WORKPLACE BULLYING
=======================
become bully-proof and regain control of your life

intro
	- workplace bullying is a fact of life
	- learn how to:
		- protect yourself against bullying behavior at the time of an attack
		- understand the ways in which being targeted may have affected what you think, feel and believe, as well as how you behave at work
		- prevent a workplace bully from successfully grooming or targeting you in the future
		- re-gain your confidence after and experience of workplace bullying
		- support someone you know who is vulnerable to being targeted, and doesn't know what to do to protect themselves
	- skills:
		- develop a bully-proof mindset which will prevent you from being successfully targeted in the future
		- improve your levels of resilience and you capacity to handle previously stressful bullying dynamics with ease
		- confront byllying behaviour quickly and effectively, clarify the boundaries and put the relevant issue back to the bully
	- patterns:
		- the bully using aggressive, intimidating behaviour towards you
		- you feeling afraid and being unable to say what you want to say, or do what you want to
		- thy bully learning that their use of aggression successfully controls you to some extent, which encourages them to use these tactics again
		- a cycle of behaviour developing between the two of you whereby the bully intimidates you, you outwardly comply with their wishes, but inwardly fume
	- you may feel distressed and overwhelmed when an attack hits you
	- furious, hurt, afraid, blaming yourself, castigated yourself, 

	- time to reflect on your self-image:
		- relevant input to challenge the unhalpful, negative believs and thought you have generated about yourself as a result of being subject to bullying behaviour
		- suggestions for effective mental skills and strategies which will enable you to breate a more positive self-image to replace the toxic shame you have generated because you were bullied
	- you need to reject the negative beliefs you have developed about yourself as a result of being bullied.
	
	- encourage you to:
		- identigy bullying behaviour at work early on, so you can mentally prepare for an attach should you be targeted
		- select effective inter-personal strategies for retaining control when you are attacked
		- understand why these strategies and tactics are effective
		- understand the dynamic at the heart of a workplace bullying relationship, so you can prevent it from being created or iterrupt it should it already be in place
		- recognize the self-defeating patterns of thinking and behaviour which you may have adopted as a result of being subject to bullying behaviour
		- understand why colleagues who witness bullying behaviour often don't spark up in support of the person being targeted
		- recognise why influential people in organisations so often fail to tackle bullying behabiour and thereby enable it
